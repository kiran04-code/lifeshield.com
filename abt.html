About

Vaccination is one of the best methods to stop the spread of infectious diseases. However, keeping track of vaccination schedules can be tough due to missed doses, incomplete records, and lack of reminders. This platform is designed to assist individuals and families in managing their vaccination schedules and providing timely reminders for upcoming doses.

The goal of this project is to allow users to register and manage their vaccination records, alert them about upcoming or overdue vaccinations via email or SMS, and display nearby vaccination centers. This initiative will increase awareness about the importance of various vaccines and emerging diseases, contributing to better public health outcomes.

Our vaccination management system offers a range of features to ensure a seamless and informed immunization experience. With Vaccination Reminders, users receive timely alerts about upcoming doses, preventing missed vaccinations and ensuring complete immunization. The Vaccination History Tracking feature allows users to store and manage their vaccination records digitally, making it easy to access and stay organized. Additionally, the Nearby Vaccination Centers feature enables users to find and book appointments at convenient locations using a location-based search. To support informed decision-making, our system provides Reliable Vaccine Information, offering detailed insights about various vaccines, their benefits, and recommended schedules.
